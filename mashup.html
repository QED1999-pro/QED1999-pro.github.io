Pinhead Patrick Looking into M.C. Escher's Ball

Since I adore Spongebob Squarepants and the countless memes that continue to develop from it, I decided why not combine a Spongebob meme with a meme from one of my favorite artists and their pieces. I decided to use Hand with Reflecting Sphere with the words "Beauty is in the eyes of the ball holder". Using photoshop techniques and tools, I replaced Escher himself in the reflection of the ball with the Pinhead Patrick meme, and then replaced Escher's hand with Patrick's. I thought that this perfectly and hilariously captured Patrick's facial expression when he looks at his own reflection.
